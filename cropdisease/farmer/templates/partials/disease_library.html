Crop Diseases
Tomato - Late Blight

Phytophthora infestans

High Severity
Symptoms

Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves, white fungal growth on undersides, and fruit rot.

Causes

Fungal infection favored by cool, wet conditions.

Prevention
  • Crop rotation
  • Proper spacing for air circulation
  • Avoid overhead watering
Treatment
  • Apply fungicides (e.g., chlorothalonil)
  • Remove and destroy affected plant parts
  • Reduce watering
Wheat - Rust

Puccinia spp.

Medium Severity
Symptoms

Orange or yellow pustules on leaves and stems, reduced photosynthesis.

Causes

Fungal spores spread by wind, thrive in warm, humid conditions.

Prevention
  • Use resistant varieties
  • Timely sowing to avoid peak infection periods
  • Monitor weather conditions
Treatment
  • Apply sulfur-based fungicides
  • Crop rotation
  • Remove crop residue
Corn - Common Smut

Ustilago maydis

Low Severity
Symptoms

Galls or tumors on ears, tassels, or stalks, filled with black spores.

Causes

Fungal infection through wounds or silks, often in warm, dry conditions.

Prevention
  • Use resistant hybrids
  • Avoid mechanical injury to plants
  • Balanced fertilization
Treatment
  • Remove and destroy galls before they rupture
  • No effective chemical control
  • Maintain plant health
Potato - Early Blight

Alternaria solani

Medium Severity
Symptoms

Concentric rings ("target spots") on leaves, premature defoliation.

Causes

Fungal infection in warm, wet conditions, spread by spores.

Prevention
  • Crop rotation
  • Use certified disease-free seed potatoes
  • Ensure proper air circulation
Treatment
  • Apply copper-based fungicides
  • Remove infected plant debris
  • Maintain soil health
Rice - Blast

Magnaporthe oryzae

High Severity
Symptoms

Grayish-white lesions with brown borders on leaves, neck rot, and panicle loss.

Causes

Fungal infection in high humidity and warm temperatures.

Prevention
  • Use resistant varieties
  • Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilization
  • Ensure proper water management
Treatment
  • Apply systemic fungicides
  • Remove infected plant parts
  • Reduce nitrogen application
